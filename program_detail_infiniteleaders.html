The Infinite Leader - Full Personalised Executive Coaching Program

Running in 3 month (8 Sessions) blocks, this is a highly personalised one-on-one coaching program for CEO's and Business Leaders that expands on the foundations of the "Live Mindfully, Lead Better" workshops, and allows for longer-term strategic implementations including:

  • -- you will develop mindfulness as a habit with practical activities that are relevant, fun and deeply insightful,
  • -- you will break through personal barriers and limitations,
  • -- you will become calmer, healthier, more in control and better equipped to deal with the unexpected,
  • -- you will become more creative, open and engaged,
  • -- you will be mentored and equipped to continue on the journey beyond the program,and much more.

This program is both physical and mental, and will challenge you in unexpected ways, inspire you and recharge you. It is available in a personalised individual coaching program, and group (min. 4 participants) coaching. People who have participated in the Introduction program may be eligible to a discount.

Ongoing individually tailored coaching packages are available for those who wish to further develop in this area.

Live Mindfully, Lead Better: 1-Day Intensive Workshop

An intensive and information rich 1-day group workshop designed to give you an experiential appreciation and become familiar with relevant mindfulness concepts, experience the physical and psychological effect of meditation and mindfulness, understand basic methods for meditation and mindfulness practice, and acquire an overview into the application and practice.

This program is run in groups of strictly limited numbers to ensure all participants get full benefit and attention. Corporate and group booking welcome. Indivuals should register their interest for the next general workshops.
