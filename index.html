Live Mindfully, Lead Better

Mindful people make extraordinary leaders, are more Innovative and influential, are contagious with happiness, are connected and compassionate, and live intensely fulfilled lives.

Winfried Sitte

Mindful Speaker. Mindful Mentor, Mindful Coach


Born in Venezuela, he emigrated with his parents and siblings to Australia as a teenager. After graduating and practicing for two decades as an Architect, he decided to combine his passion for music, Zen and personal development with his expertise in design processes to help people reclaim control and become deliberate about their life. He currently lives in Brisbane with his soul-mate and their two children.

Winfried attacks life with passion and enthusiasm. He has a great ability to view issues from various angles and to investigate the opportunities presented. In particular, he considers multiple aspects and criteria to his solutions rather than the first or most obvious. Expect to be challenged by him.

— Natalie Dau, CEO (Singapore)
